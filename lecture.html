SMBE Conference Presentation

At the Society for Molecular Biology and Evolution (SMBE) 2024, I presented a poster on "Genomic Insights into the Evolutionary Trajectory of Antibiotic Resistance in Klebsiella spp. and Escherichia coli: A Study from Central Mexico (2020-2023)." My presentation highlighted our study on the evolutionary dynamics of multi-drug and pan-drug-resistant strains of Klebsiella spp. and Escherichia coli. We identified and characterized resistant strains, revealing an alarming increase in resistance gene diversity and prevalence over the years. The study emphasized the need for ongoing genomic surveillance to adapt treatment strategies and combat the growing threat of antibiotic resistance.
